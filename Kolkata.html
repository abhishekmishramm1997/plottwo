BOTTOM SHELF BRANDY


Maidan, Kolkata.

     

     

Through all the places that I discovered in West Bengal in the all the time that I had, one song kept me company: Abhi Na Jao Chhor Kar Ki Dil Abhi Bhara Nahi. I felt in every moment that the divine-however we may choose to define such a thing-surely dwells as much in the concrete and taxi cabs as it does in the rivers, lakes, and mountains. Grace, I realised, is neither time nor place dependent. All we need is the right soundtrack. Even though, I am terrible at singing, I still humned to its tunes as I found my way through narrowed lanes, lanes that my senses gradually grew accustomed to, and my head to bended as I greeted old tea-sellers and vendors with a smile.
