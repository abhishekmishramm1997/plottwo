STRANGENESS


Words fall out

from the mouth

of a foreigner

living deep inside me

     

words pour out

from a nameless river

flowing deep within me

     

an empty silence

basked in music

a blank canvas

drenched in poetry

     

an obscure struggle

to say something beyond

the words put into my mouth

to exist as somebody beyond

a foreigner in my own world

     

blindfolded

my hands reach out

to a world I cannot see

quivering in the dark

quivering at the thought

of strangeness

engulfing me

     

i stand still

breathing

hearing the sound

of my beating heart

     

wondering if the dust ever settles

wondering if it’s a mere consolation

     

a sloppy design of handpicked dots joined together

in a pattern my eyes can bear to see

     

wondering about the strange need

to matter where nothing can

     

wondering about the search for truth

in a world where people can’t seem

to say what they mean or

mean what they say

     

and

     

wondering still,

if people can ever escape

what they know to be true deep inside

     

– Ananya
